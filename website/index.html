Introducing SceneIt

An easier way of ordering tickets

SceneIt is an app created by Group 8 - A02 that is meant for theatre lovers. Our goal with SceneIt is to provide users with the ability to order theatre tickets from a mobile device - allowing them to choose where, when and what they watch. SceneIt aims to make this process easier by showing users a variety of filtered catalogues depending on location, time and current trends.

If you’d like to check out an overview of SceneIt and it’s purpose head on over to the “Overview” section! If you want to get some info on how SceneIt was created, be sure to check out the “Developmental Process” blog.
