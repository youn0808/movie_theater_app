SceneIt Overview

An app made for theatre goers

Vision Statement & Overview

We have chosen to build a movie ticket purchasing application for our project. Our users will be able to purchase and reserve movie tickets through an app before going to the theater.

What is it?

SceneIt is a one-stop-shop to purchase movie tickets at our user’s convenience. The app stores useful information such as the latest movie releases, theater locations, airing times as well as ratings. After purchasing a movie ticket, users will receive their receipt through email and provide it at the theater. SceneIt aims to make the ticket ordering process easier by showing users a variety of filtered catalogues depending on location, time and current trends.

Who is it for?

SceneIt was created with theatre lovers in mind. Our goal with SceneIt is to provide users with the ability to order theatre tickets from a mobile device - allowing them to choose where, when and what they watch. Our goal is to connect those who want to watch movies - to theatres who are presenting movies

Why should I download this?

The SceneIt app is an improvement from the traditional method of purchasing a movie ticket. With the app users are not required to wait in lines to purchase their tickets before the movie air time anymore. With the app, moviegoers are allowed an preemptive and convenient way of purchasing movie tickets online… before the tickets are sold out!. Users value their time, and so the simplistic and intuitive design of the SceneIt app allows any user to order tickets online.

Additionally, the app is serves as a valuable marketing tool to theaters as it can assist in attracting new and loyal users by promotion via AppStore or Google Play. Not all theatres have the luxury of commissioning their own online ticket ordering app, although through partnership with SceneIt they can still realize the benefits of online ordering.
